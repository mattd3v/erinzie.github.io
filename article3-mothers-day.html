Mother's Day Gift List for Moms of All Types!

With Mother’s Day less than a week away and quarantine still in affect I felt this would be a great year to go over some thoughtful gifts for moms with all sorts of passions and styles! With so many people stuck at home right now, shine a light on all the things your mom or wife loves this year and be ready to go through the most extensive gift guide you’ve ever seen!

Food & Beverages

If she enjoys eating more than cooking, treat her to a nice restaurant for dinner! If you're in an area that's currently sheltering in place then you can support your local restaurants and order in!
If she enjoys cooking, try to bond over cooking or baking with her. You can grab the ingredients as a surprise or pick them up with her!
Infused Oils -- If you can't go out to do something with her right now try gifts like these infused cooking oils! Sure to take her dishes to the next level!
A good knife set -- I never realized how important this is until I moved recently and was stuck with some really awful knives that made me so frustrated anytime I had to cut things like meat, potatos, or melons. If your wife/mom could use some new knives to make cooking those delicous home made meals easier than get her one of these high quality sets.
New baking sets -- Maybe your mom likes to bake, but could use some new equiptment. Show her you notice with these nonstick pans and heat resistant oven mits.
Bread making tools -- Does your mom like doing things the old fashioned way? Surprise her with a bread making kit! She'll love to learn how to make her own bread and her own original recipes!
Cheese making -- If she's a fan of cheese (aren't we all) then I bet she'd love to learn how to make her own cheese! Gift her with one of these kits to unleash her cheese making potentials.
A wine or beer tasting -- If she isn't huge on food, but finds herself to be somewhat of a connoisseur, have a wine tasting with her, and she prefers beer over wine have a flight with her!
Wine accessories -- Here are somethings to go with a nice bottle of her favorite wine.
Home brewing -- If she has the patience to wait the brewing period, nothing feels more accomplishing than seasoning and brewing your own drinks!
Wine making -- Here are some options for making your own wine. I've recently seen that you can make your own wine out of dandelion / clover flowers, how fem!
Mixology -- Is your mom an amatuer bartender? Give her the tools she deserves to step her game up and get mixing like a pro!

Miscellaneous

If you picked miscellaneous, try spending some personal time with your mom this Mother’s Day.
